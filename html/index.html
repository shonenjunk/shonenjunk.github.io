Who we are

Black Sheep Egg Company is a farmer-owned, farmer-operated egg processing and packing company located in Northeast Arkansas. Our farms are scattered throughout the pasture belt running through Arkansas and Missouri. We are unique in that we process and package the eggs that are produced by the hens on our farms. We are composed of 8 farm families, and collectively, we have over 100 years experience in the poultry industry and almost 350 years experience in agriculture.

Farmer Owned Company

At Black Sheep we pride ourselves on being a 100% Farmer owned and operated company. We built our company from the ground up wit ha misssion to bring you the best free range eggs in the Galaxy, without the inlfuence of Big Corp. Our chicks live on small family farms scattered across the midwest, where mild winters, and long sunny summer days allow our chicks to roam outside all year long. The only cages you'll find around here are the ones the farmers end up in when we spend too much time with the wrong chicks.

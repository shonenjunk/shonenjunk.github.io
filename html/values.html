Our Values

  1. Farmers – Farmers are the heart of our operation, and we believe farmer input is critical and necessary for long-term success. Our company is both farmer-owned and farmer-managed to ensure a direct and integral connection to farmers.
  2. Family – Our farms are centered around the traditional farm family model in which all family members share in farm responsibilities and thus develop time-honored traits such as accountability, integrity, and dignity in work.
  3. The American Dream – We ascribe to the ideals of the American Dream, believing that all people should have equal opportunity to achieve success and prosperity through hard work, determination, and initiative.
  4. Equity in the Supply Chain – We believe that every link in the supply chain should have the opportunity to make a fair profit based upon the merits of each link’s contributions.
  5. Responsible Stewardship – We acknowledge that it is our responsibility to effectively care for the resources involved in our operations in a way that promotes good stewardship and sustainability.

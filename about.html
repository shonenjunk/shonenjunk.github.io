About Us

Black Sheep Egg Company is a farmer-owned, farmer-operated egg processing and packing company located in Northeast Arkansas. Our farms are scattered throughout the pasture belt running through Arkansas and Missouri. We are unique in that we process and package the eggs that are produced by the hens on our farms. We are composed of 8 farm families, and collectively, we have over 100 years experience in the poultry industry and almost 350 years experience in agriculture.
